          About

          Welcome to Landing Zone Assistant

          Use the get location button in the tools tab to get your exact location and radio the coordinates to the pilot. Be sure to use the checklist to ensure a safe landing zone. Switch on your flashlight to help see at night. Use the overhead view map to see what the pilot will most likely see when approaching. If available, call your local flight service using the directory.
